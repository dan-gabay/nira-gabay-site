## What NOT to do (verified against 2026 Hebrew sources + Google spam docs)

1. **Never buy links.** Cheap Hebrew links (under ~$50) are PBN inventory; Google's SpamBrain (incl. Aug-2025 update) ignores or penalizes them, and the lost benefit is NOT recoverable after cleanup.
2. **No mass directory dumps** - the "400-600 אינדקסים" submission packages are link-farm footprints. Only niche-relevant, editorially moderated indexes (this plan's list).
3. **No keyword anchors** in directories - brand anchors only.
4. **No advertorials-as-SEO** - sponsored placements are nofollow by policy.
5. **Directory quality rubric** (from Hebrew SEO practitioners): real users? editorial approval? ratings/filtering? organic traffic? If none - skip.
